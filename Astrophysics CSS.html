Astrophysics

What is Astrophysics?

Astrophysics is a branch of science that applies the principles of physics and chemistry to study celestial objects and phenomena. It explores the universe, including stars, galaxies, planets, and the large-scale structure of the cosmos, investigating their origins, evolution, and physical properties.
